About Dr. Mahasweta M Dass

Music is the essence of life and for every season and every reason, it exists in the core of every species and human being, therefore is universal. Dr. Mahasweta M Dass is a global music teacher, mentor, collaborator, guide, and a researcher. She is a doctorate in Indian Classical Music from the University of Delhi, has trained more than 10,000 music passionate people and children across the globe through her affiliations with renowned schools, international boards, and through her online curated programs and classes. Her training goes beyond the confinement of languages, genre, styles, or culture, and with her contributions extends close to 3 decades. She through her music initiative Vividha has developed her own school of thought, of training & inspiring the young global community of children and adults to learn music in an extremely versatile, comprehensive manner, thereby taking this art form ahead to many and across the generations. Her unique teaching curriculum and methodology are attracting international students from all walks of life.

What Motivates Me

Singing is a fearless expression of one's innermost emotions. Dr. Mahasweta has been training the music lovers considering their objectives, musical tastes, interests, and aptitude through curated activities, training, and lesson plans. In one class, you might get to see her training western music and in another class she is chanting the Vedic mantras or breaking into an opera or a jazz song yet the fundamentals of her music remain the same. It is her ideology that is attracting the international students, who join her from across the country, Australia, New Zealand, Europe, USA, UAE, Singapore, and Asia Pacific. She has students coming to her from all walks of life, across generations with myriad schools of thoughts, musical understanding, learning objectives, interests, and aptitude. Her methodology has been simplistic and through her online classes, she is on her mission for spreading the love and light of this art form, thereby creating Artists, music healers, crusaders, and world-class music entrepreneurs. She has named her music training initiative and lesson plan as VIVIDHA, meaning the diversity.
